Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is a classical dessert which is enjoyed worldwide, making it a recipe worth trying out!
It doesn't take many ingredients, but requires good execution in order to be delicious.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese : 160z
  • Graham crackers : 8 crackers
  • Butter(melted) : 1tbs
  • Eggs : 3
  • Vanilla Extract : 1tsp
  • Condensed Milk : 1 can

Steps

  1. Grind up your graham crackers along with the melted butter and use it to create a crust at the bottom of your pan.
  2. In a separate bowl, using an electic hand mixer, mix the eggs and cream cheese.
  3. Once combined, add in your Condensed Milk and mix until no lumps are found.
  4. Preheat oven to 350 F
  5. While preheating, pour your cream cheese mixture onto your crust and place in the oven for 30 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
