Cereal

Description

Cereal is the world's fastest breakfast and can really save you some time in thsoe busy early mornings.
It is a simple recipe and easy for beginner cooks, all while being delicious and customizable.
The only requirement for a good bowl of cereal is to have un-expired milk, and a good cereal brand.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Your cereal of choice

Steps

  1. Pour in your desired amount of cereal into a bowl leaving 1/2 inch or more of room for your milk.
  2. Pour in your milk until all your cereal bits are floating.
  3. Enjoy!
